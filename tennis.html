Serena Williams

Has won 73 career singles titles, 23 doubles titles and two mixed doubles titles. This includes 39 Grand Slam titles - 23 singles titles, 14 doubles titles and two mixed doubles titles. Four-time Olympic gold medalist.


Andy Murray

Andy Murray, in full Sir Andrew Barron Murray, (born May 15, 1987, Glasgow, Scotland), Scottish tennis player who was one of the sport's premier players during the 2010s, winning three Grand Slam titles and two men's singles Olympic gold medals.
